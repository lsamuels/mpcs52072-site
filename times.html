    Lecture Structure¶

    You will meet with the instructor once a week during a scheduled synchronous Zoom session. These sessions will be used to cover new material, review concepts from the pre-recorded lectures, work through exercises, answer questions, and facilitate discussion.

    This course will be fully remote during the summer. All synchronous class meetings will take place live on Zoom. Information on how to access the Zoom sessions will be provided through Canvas.

    Most weeks, there will be a series of pre-recorded lectures that you must watch before attending the synchronous class session. These videos constitute the asynchronous portion of the course and are designed to prepare you for the weekly live discussions and activities.

    More details are provided in the syllabus.
